The Asian Food Experience

Join us on a gastronomical adventure through the 48 countries in the Asian continent.

Our Inspiration

At 48 East, we want to unravel several stories—one of each of the 48 countries in Asia. These are stories that have been twisted and lost over the years as Manchurian and Noodles slowly and solely claimed all of Asian cuisine. What if we told you there is more food to be explored?

We bring to you a wide array of Asian flavours, straight from the heart of 48 different countries. From Gyoza to Khow suey to the most succulent Iranian kebabs, each dish is cherry-picked and prepared using traditional methods by the experienced hands in our kitchen. Our mission is to create magic by delighting your palate with unexplored flavours. Let us give you the true Asian story—one that will stay with you forever.

Every week a fresh spread, everyday a celebration of good food. Brace yourselves as we unravel the story of Asia.

Think food. Think East.

Specially curated parcels of love by our experienced hands at the kitchen.

Meet Our Team

Joseph Cherian
Founder and CEO

A travel aficionado who loves experimenting with everything Asian, Joseph has worked with 4 brands in 7 Asian countries. He's an alum of Harvard Business School. He has served as the CEO of Papa Johns India and the Director of GFA Global.

Nabhojit Ghosh
Co-Founder

As a global culinary professional with experience of over 30 years working with leading hotels in India and abroad, Chef Nabhojit loves experimenting with ingredients and creating interesting food. He has served as the Executive Chef in Oberoi, Radisson and Taj Hotels, and also, as the Corporate Chef Trainer in Oberoi School of Learning and Taj Hotels.
